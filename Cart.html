ICANN Fee
This mandatory fee is charged by ICANN (Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers: www.icann.org), and became effective on November 1, 2004.
The ICANN fee for .com, .net, .org, .biz, .info, .asia, .jobs, .xxx, .mobi, .uno, .menu, .build and .luxury is $0.18 per domain name registration year. Thus, the fee on a one year .com registration would be $0.18. The fee would be $0.36 for a two year registration (i.e. $0.18 times 2). Additional years would be at $0.18.
